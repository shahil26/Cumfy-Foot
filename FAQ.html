Cumfy Foot - Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products and add items to your cart. Once you're ready to check out, follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment information. We'll send you a confirmation email with your order details.

Can I cancel or change my order?

If you need to cancel or change your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but keep in mind that once an order has shipped, we can't make any changes.

Shipping

What are my shipping options?

We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Standard shipping is free for orders over $50 and takes 3-7 business days. Expedited shipping is available for an additional fee and takes 1-2 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please contact us for more information.

Returns

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items that are unworn and in their original packaging. You'll need to cover the cost of return shipping. Once we receive your return, we'll issue a refund to your original payment method.

What if my item is damaged or defective?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.
