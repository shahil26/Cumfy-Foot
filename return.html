Cancellation and Return Policy

At Cumfy Foot, we understand that sometimes you may need to cancel or return an order. We aim to make this process as easy as possible for you.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your order at any time before it has been shipped. Once your order has been shipped, you will not be able to cancel it.

Return Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at customerservice@cumfyfoot.com and provide your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.

Please note that the item(s) must be returned in their original packaging and in the same condition as when you received them. Once we receive your returned item(s), we will inspect them and process your refund or exchange within 5 business days.
