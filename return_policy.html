Return Policy

At Cumfy Foot, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return your purchase within 30 days of the original purchase date for a full refund or exchange.

All returned items must be in their original condition and packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Please contact us at returns@cumfyfoot.com to initiate a return or exchange. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions for returning the item.

If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
